- Exports **`welcomeUserWorkflow`** (`'welcome-user'`) with two steps:
  1. **`notify-in-app`** (`step.inApp`) – sends a personalised in-app notification.
  2. **`send-email`** (`step.email`) – sends a personalised HTML welcome email.

### `index.js`
- Creates an Express app on **port 3000**.
- Mounts `express.json()` middleware (required by the bridge handler).
- Registers the Novu Bridge Endpoint at **`/api/novu`** via `serve({ workflows: [welcomeUserWorkflow] })`.
- Exposes a health-check route at `/`.
